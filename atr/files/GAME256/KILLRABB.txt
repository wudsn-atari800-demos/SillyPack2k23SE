
 ================ Epic Killer Rabbits Battle! - a 256 bytes game for Atari XL/XE by Altair ================

        BOOT WITH BASIC!!! (NO OPTION KEY)


Objective:
   Exterminate opposing rabbit army!
   Devour carrots to multiply!
   Rush on enemy rabbit for them to clash to death (and let a new carrot grow on their decomposing bodies!)
   Watch for relentless foxes!

   The battle zone is always symmetrical, so no whining about uneven chances!
   Maps found under keys 0..9 & A..Z all have passed quality check in regards to carrot/foxes amounts and their topology.


Controls:
   Joy 1                   - Move green rabbits army
   Joy 2                   - Move viole(n)t rabbits army
   0..9/A..Z               - Select one of the premium 36 maps & restart (QA certified!)
   Shift+0..9/A..Z         - More maps! (some may be unplayable)
   Ctrl+0..9/A..Z          - More maps! (some may be unplayable)
   Shift+Ctrl+0..9/A..Z    - More maps! (some may be unplayable)


Have fun!
